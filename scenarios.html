Stress Test Findings
What This Tells You
🔴 Critical Finding: The $70/sf hard cost escalation ($215 → $285) adds $11.6M to total project cost. Because CMHC loans against appraised value (not cost), equity requirement explodes from $3.7M → $17–21M. The deal as structured at $6.5M land does not work at market construction costs.
💡 Path to Viability: To make the deal work at $285/sf you need one of: (1) land cost reduced to ~$4.0–4.5M (vs. $6.5M ask), (2) NOI increased to push appraised value above $47.9M (requires ~35% rent increase — not realistic), or (3) JV with seller contributing land as equity to absorb the shortfall. Breakeven land price at $285/sf ≈ $4.05M.
📐 DSCR is Healthy — Equity is the Problem: All three LTV scenarios show solid DSCR (1.22x–1.38x). The project cash flows. The issue is not debt service — it's the $11.9M cost-to-value gap that must be absorbed by equity or land price compression.
