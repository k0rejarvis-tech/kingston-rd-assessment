Executive Summary

2759–2763 Kingston Road presents a purpose-built rental development in Scarborough's Cliffside neighbourhood on a 15,617 sf site. The proposed 10-storey mixed-use building accommodates 95 residential units plus 433 sm of ground-floor retail. The project qualifies for CMHC MLI Select 100-point maximum — unlocking 50-year amortization and ~90% LTV, which is the critical financing advantage that makes this deal viable.

Under the JV structure (seller contributes land as equity), developer cash requirement drops to $700K. Under the land bump strategy, net cash equity reduces to $1.7M. Conventional financing fails entirely at 0.92x DSCR.
